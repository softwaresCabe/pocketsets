Common Issues

  • Notifications not working Check your device notification settings for PocketSets.
  • App crashes Try restarting the app or your device.
  • Data not syncing Check your internet connection and try again.
  • Tip purchase failed Check your payment method and internet connection. Tips are processed through the App Store or Google Play.
  • Restore purchases Tips are consumable and don't need restoration. If you have issues, contact support.
