Privacy Policy

PocketSets  ·  Last updated: April 22, 2026

Overview

PocketSets is an unofficial fan-made festival companion app. We are committed to your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data except for anonymous tip analytics. Everything the app does stays on your device, with optional tip purchases processed through secure third-party services.

Data We Do Not Collect

PocketSets does not collect, transmit, or share:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any information about the artists or sets you favorite

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device and never leaves it:

  • Favorites — sets you star are saved in the app's local database
  • Settings — notification lead time, theme preference, and simulated time are saved in local app storage
  • Widget data — your next favorited set and live stage info are written to a shared App Group on-device so the home screen widget can display them

None of this data is backed up to any server we operate.

Notifications

PocketSets uses iOS local notifications to remind you before favorited sets start. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device — no notification passes through any external server. You can disable them at any time in your iOS Settings.

Tips and Payments

PocketSets offers optional tip purchases to support development. Tips are processed through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android) and RevenueCat. Payment information is handled securely by these platforms — we never see or store your payment details.

When you complete a tip, only the USD amount is anonymously tracked for analytics purposes. This helps us understand which tip tiers are most popular. No personal information is associated with these events.

Festival Schedule Data

The app fetches the EDC Las Vegas 2026 schedule from a read-only server to populate artist and set information. This request does not include any personal information — it is equivalent to loading a public web page.

Third-Party Services

PocketSets integrates with the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — Handles in-app tip purchases securely through the App Store or Google Play. Payment information is processed by Apple/Google and RevenueCat according to their privacy policies.
  • PostHog — Anonymous analytics for tip completion events (only the USD amount is tracked). No personal data is collected. You can opt out by not using the tip feature.

These services help support the development of PocketSets while keeping it free and ad-free.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy
